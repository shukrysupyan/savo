About Us

Savorè Eatery, where every bite tells a story of comfort, creativity, and connection. At Savorè, we blend the charm of European pastries with the heartiness of Asian-inspired mains and the flair of modern, handcrafted drinks — all thoughtfully curated to delight every plate.

Got Questions?

Is your food halal?

Yes, all ingredients and meats used at Savorè Eatery are sourced from halal-certified suppliers.

Do you take reservations?

We operate mostly on a walk-in basis, but reservations are available for groups of 4 and above. Kindly contact us in advance.

Are your pastries baked fresh daily?

Absolutely! Our pastries, including mini quiches, pain au chocolat and pannetone are baked fresh every morning.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, we have vegetarian-friendly dishes like our Chickpea & Spinach Curry with Rice
